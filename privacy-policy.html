Privacy & Policy

Your data privacy is our top priority. We provide professional cyber café services with transparency.

At Digital World Cyber Café, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our free and paid services such as browsing, gaming, printing, ticketing, and more.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, password
  • Payment details for services purchased
  • Session and usage data (time, duration, activity logs)
  • User content: uploaded files, bookings, photos
  • Cookies & local storage for personalization

How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process secure payments
  • Provide software updates and technical support
  • Analyze usage to enhance performance and security
  • Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and misuse

Sharing & Third-Party Services

  • Trusted partners for payments, analytics, and communication
  • No selling of personal data to third parties
  • Information may be shared only if required by law or government authorities

User Rights

  • Access, update, or correct personal information
  • Request deletion of data where legally applicable
  • Opt-out of marketing emails and notifications
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability)

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security Measures

Your data is protected using encryption, secure sessions, and periodic audits. Sensitive information such as payments are processed through secure channels.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
